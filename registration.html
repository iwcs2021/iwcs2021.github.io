Registration

You can still register! But no longer from this page. Contact iwcs2021@gmail.com for the registration link. This is to ensure we immediately know about your registration and can forward you the information on how to participate.

Registration gives you full online access to the main conference and all colocated workshops. The registration fee is 40 euro (~50 USD). You can pay through iDeal, VISA or MasterCard. Please contact us if you have problems with the payment.
