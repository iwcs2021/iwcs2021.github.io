Call for workshop proposals

We invite proposals for workshops to be held in conjunction with IWCS 2021. We solicit proposals in all areas of computational semantics, in other words all computational aspects of meaning of natural language within written, spoken, signed, or multi-modal communication. Workshops are invited on these closely related topics.

Important Dates

  • 8 January 2021: Workshop proposal submissions due
  • 15 January 2021: Workshop proposal notification of acceptance
All deadlines are "anywhere on earth".

Submission information

Proposals for workshops should contain:

  • A title and brief (max two pages) description of the workshop topic and content
  • An estimate of the expected audience size
  • If the workshop has been held before, a note specifying where previous workshops were held, how many submissions the workshop received, how many papers were accepted and how many attendees the workshop attracted
  • The names, affiliation and email addresses of the organisers
Submissions should be submitted to iwcs2021@gmail.com.

After acceptance

Organisers of accepted workshops will be contacted regarding further steps in the process. There will be close contact regarding the likely online nature of the conference.

Finances

Workshops must be financially self-supporting. The conference organisers will establish registration rates to cover the costs.
